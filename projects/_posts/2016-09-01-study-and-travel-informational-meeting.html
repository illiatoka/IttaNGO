Every few month NGO ITTA organizes informational meetings “Study and travel” where we introduce Erasmus+ Programme of the European Union and talk about learning opportunities it offers to young people.

During the workshop we talk about:

  • What is Erasmus + Programme, key actions of Erasmus +
  • What is non-formal education
  • What is EVS and how to participate
  • How to write a CV
  • Tips on writing a perfect motivation letter
