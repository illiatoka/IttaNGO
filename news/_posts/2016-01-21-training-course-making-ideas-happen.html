NGO ITTA is looking for 2 Ukrainian participants for the training course "Making ideas happen", which will be held on 1 - 7 April 2016 in Larnaca, Cyprus.
Making Ideas Happen is a training course focusing on entrepreneurship using innovative methods that are already tested in the business world.

During the training youth workers, youth leaders and volunteers will learn how to design and implement entrepreneurial activities and how to build their own business.

The project aims in:

  • developing the capacity of youth organisations in entrepreneurship
  • strengthening the competences of youth workers to support their target-groups
  • providing the individual participants with skills necessary to succeed both in youth activities and the workplace

The selection would be done by NGO ITTA according to the profile of the participants. Preference will be given to active youth workers/leaders.

The flights and accommodation will be covered by Erasmus+ (up to 275 EUR in Flights).
